This test measures the speed between your computer and the Internet to help you see if you have the speed you need.

Before you take the test, be sure to:

  • Connect your laptop or computer directly to your modem or router
  • Limit the number of applications and devices using your Internet connection
  • Disconnect from any proxy connection, such as a Virtual Private Network (VPN)
